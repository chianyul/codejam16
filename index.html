About

CodeJam is a 48-hour team-based programming competition fueled by great minds and energy drinks.

McGill University's Electrical, Computer and Software Engineering Student Society (ECSESS) is collaborating with some of Montreal's biggest software companies to make a challenging, real-world application problem for you to solve. ECSESS's largest event is back for its 7th year and this time it's all about Human Computer Interaction.

The CodeJam Committee

Anthony - Chair

Anthony is a 4th year electrical engineering student. His passion for the physical sciences, especially physics, is rivaled only by his headstrong attitude when it comes to challenges and problem solving. A space and astronomy enthusiast, Anthony’s eventual goal in life is to become a full time astronaut. For now, though, he is excited to be the Chair for this year’s CodeJam. Anthony also enjoys building things, eating jam and reading books on his spare time.

Alina - Sponsorship

Alina is currently in her third year in Computer Engineering at McGill. In addition to being in charge of the sponsorship for Codejam 2016 she is also VP External of ECSESS (Electrical, Computer, Software Engineering Student Society). Her eventual goal in life is to be a professional jam maker but for now she will settle with engineering.

Erick - Merchandising

Erick is currently in his second year in Software Engineering at McGill. When he’s not toiling his life away in the windowless dungeon that is the DPM lab, he enjoys procrastinating by posting up on scrubs in 2K in the ECSESS lounge. Back when he didn’t wake up in cold chills to the sound of EV3 runtime errors ringing in his head, he would enjoy making collages on Photoshop and honing his front-end web dev skills. If you would like to dust off those long-forgotten relics of a bygone era, you could visit his tumblr blog or check out his personal web page.

Michel - Design

Michel is a first-year Electrical Engineering student passionate about coding, robotics and Dank Memes. When he isn’t working he is procrastinating with Netflix or using his Official Froshie Of The Year 2016 title. Master of wake up without hangovers.

Suleman - Food

Suleman is in his second year of Software Engineering at Mcgill. Suleman, also known as “The People's U2 Software Rep” can usually be found in the lounge destroying Erick at FIFA and Call of Duty. When he’s not in the lounge he is most likely making gains in the gym. He also enjoys cooking/eating dope food (that’s why he is VP of Food). He also likes programming in Java and making iOS apps in Swift. Hopefully he will become the first ever programmer to deadlift 5 plates!
